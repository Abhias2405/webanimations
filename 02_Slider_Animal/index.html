MOOSE
The moose is known for its immense strength, endurance, and resilience in the wild. As one of the largest members of the deer family, a moose symbolizes power, confidence, and the ability to navigate through tough environments with grace and authority. Its sturdy build and large antlers make it a symbol of defense and protection, ideal for representing secure, strong systems in a digital world.

ELEPHANT
The elephant is a symbol of wisdom, strength, and memory. Known for its immense size and powerful presence, elephants are natural protectors of their herd, and their ability to remember long distances and routes makes them a perfect symbol of reliable security and long-lasting protection. With a tough hide and formidable tusks, the elephant represents an unbreakable defense and stable support.

ANIMAL
The tiger is a symbol of raw power, agility, and precision. With its sharp claws, quick reflexes, and strategic hunting skills, the tiger represents a perfect balance between speed and strength. Known for its ability to act both silently and decisively, the tiger stands for stealth, power, and effective protection against threats.
